What's the point of all this?

That was something that went through my head as I continued to work through web design. I felt confident at the beginning, learning how to make a fairly decent website using only the basic building blocks. However, my confidence quickly shattered once I tried to wrap my brain around features like flexbox and grid. It almost felt at though my brain was melting trying my hardest to understand new concepts while simultaneously implementing them into my sites. Even small details like applying color felt like the rug was being swept underneath me, throwing out everything I thought I once knew. Many times I felt as though I wanted to quit. But once things started to click, I couldn't help myself with all the fun I was having. As long as I kept pushing through the late nights and rough days, I felt myself growing in my ability to make some pretty good websites (if I do say so myself). And now, I can't wait to see what I can cook up next!
